CONTROL BOX SETUP GUIDE

Please read each step carefully.

1. Insert x2 AA Batteries

  • Open the battery compartment on the back by lifting and rotating.
  • Insert the alkaline AA batteries with the correct polarity (-/+).
  • Close the battery compartment cover.

2. Verify Power

  • Press the bottom-right most button to wake up the box.
  • The two status LEDs on the front should be on (red and blue).
  • If they are not then Verify Step 1 was completed properly.

3. Plug-in USB Dongle

  • Find a working USB port on your PC (front, back or top).
  • Plug in the dongle.
  • If the PC is on the red light should turn off.
  • If the red light is still on verify that the USB port works.
HELP: Not Connecting

4. Download Software

  • Visit our download page by clicking the button below.
Download Software

5. Open the Zip File and Installer

  • Open the extracted folder by double left-clicking.
  • Run the setup installer (.exe)
  • Complete all the steps in the installer.
  • Open Control Box Configurator on your desktop.

6. Application Setup

  1. Accept the End User License Agreement.
  2. If a popup says no box connected then ensure Steps 1-3 are complete.
  3. Select your application choice on the bottom (GSPro is the default).
  4. Click the white buttons in the application to change its action.

7. Adjust Settings

  • Open the settings in the top-left corner of the application.
  • You can adjust the volume and sound effects to you liking.
