About Us

SANCTION is a solo-developed indie game created by Leland Carter, a Computer Scientist from Halifax, Nova Scotia.

With a passion for procedural generation and immersive gameplay, Leland has crafted a unique post-apocalyptic experience that challenges players to survive in a harsh, ever-changing world.

As the founder of Lethal Lizard Studios, Leland handles all aspects of development including programming, design, and implementation, bringing his vision to life independently.
